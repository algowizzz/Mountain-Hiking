Mountain Hiking Tips

  • Always check the weather forecast before starting your hike.
  • Wear comfortable and sturdy hiking boots.
  • Start early to avoid hiking in the dark.
  • Stay on marked trails to avoid getting lost.
  • Carry enough water and snacks to stay hydrated and energized.

Backpacking Essentials

  • Durable backpack with a rain cover.
  • First aid kit for emergencies.
  • Map and compass or GPS device.
  • Extra layers of clothing for warmth.
  • Multi-tool or Swiss Army knife.
